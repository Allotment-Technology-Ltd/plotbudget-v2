# What's new

Recent updates to PLOT. We ship regularly to make budgeting together simpler and more reliable.

---

## February 2026

### 1.4 — Native app feel

- **Mobile app:** Smoother, more native experience with updated bottom sheets and small interaction tweaks so the app feels more responsive in your hand.

### 1.3 — First 50 free and founding spots

- **Marketing:** Clearer calls to action and a simple "first 50 free" offer so new households can try PLOT without commitment.
- **Founding spots:** We now track and surface founding-member spots via the API so we can reward early supporters.

### 1.2 — Defaults and wording

- **Settings and UI:** Updated default values and consistent wording across the app so labels and options are clearer.

### 1.1.1 — Security and reliability

- **Security:** Dependency updates and internal security improvements so your data stays safe.
- **Email:** Resend library updated for more reliable delivery of invites and notifications.

### 1.1 — Founding members

- **Founding members:** Founding member status now lives at the household level so both partners are recognised. Subscription and billing screens are tidier for founders, with a clear badge and simpler messaging.

### 1.0.1 — Polish

- **Settings:** Partner name now shows correctly in the profile tab.
- **Quality:** Various small fixes and visual tweaks for a more consistent experience.

### 1.0 — PLOT launch

- **PLOT 1.0:** We launched the first full version of PLOT: the 20-minute payday ritual for households.
- **Dashboard, Blueprint, Settings:** You can manage your budget cycle, allocate needs and wants, track savings and repayments, and see your progress at a glance.
- **Subscriptions:** Choose a plan that fits your household; checkout and billing are handled securely.
- **Cookie consent:** You can control analytics and cookie preferences from the app.
- **Founding members:** Early supporters get a thank-you message and a dedicated badge on the pricing page when they have time left on their free Premium period.
- **Currency:** Pick GBP, USD, or EUR in Household Settings; all amounts and symbols update across the app.
- **Privacy and terms:** Marketing site links to our privacy policy and terms so you know how we handle your data.
